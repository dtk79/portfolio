The Work

3M Careers

Challenge

3M is a large corproation with a number of personnel needs - many in the engineering fields. Attracting qualified applicants is a never-ending challenge. Attract new talent and increase job applications by featuring stories about real employees and the possibilities that await at 3M.

Solution

Working with Magnet 360, we developed a platform allowing us to communicate the various successes throughout the company, that one wouldn't normally hear about. We featured actual engineers who developed these technologies and showed how each made their way to everyday products found in an average home.

Additionally, we:

  • Developed a unique visual to display stories, tying together the inventor / employee, the innovation they developed and the products that resulted.
  • Created an entirely new mechanism to collect and curate mentions of 3M innovations in the news and display them to site users, demonstrating the popularity of 3M in the press.
  • Culled the existing 24 page job application process down to 7 while still meeting the demands of internal job application workflow.

Result

Increased job applications, job application conversions increased, caliber of job applicants increased.
