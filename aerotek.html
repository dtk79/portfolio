The Work

Aerotek

Challenge

Re-brand, re-architect and launch an industry-leading staffing site.

Solution

Working with Pure, I had the opportunity to do this project the right way - starting with research and interviews, concepting solutions, testing with actual site users and itterating as necessary. The result is truly an industry-first in terms of strategy and approach.

Result

The site is still in development. Initial feedback has been along the lines of "This is sensational!"

Sitemap, Templates

We had the challenge of architecting the site before its platforms had been decided on. Taking a platform-agnostic approach and proceeding on assumptions gave us room to push for what we wanted to recommend. When a platform was selected, some features were rolled back, though we still had a large universe with which to work from.

Prototyping, Testing

By conducting an extensive usability test based on an Invision prototype, we gained valuable insights that informed several revisions.
