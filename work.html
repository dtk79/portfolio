The Work

Target

I've had a lot of fun and learned LOTS while working with Target over the years on several initiatives, including:

  • Leading UX on product teams for Target.com's product recommnedations, ratings and reviews, and question and answers functionaltiy.
  • Worked among a larger team on the Product Detail Page redesign in its current form.
  • Lead UX for the virtual bundle shopping experience.
  • Worked to carry through a promotional messaging strategy to handle all forms of pricing specials, sales, clearance, coupon messaging, etc., throughout the site.
  • Consulted on a project for Target's Global Photography initiative - an effort to consolidate all digital photography work to a select few global operations centers. I worked to design the interface various users throughout the workflow would need to usher the work to the next point in the process, ultimately indexing and cataloging the assets available and allowing them to be used in digital and print forms.
